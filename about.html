About Us

We're building this with you.

We believe:

It's time for pioneers.

There's a well-worn path that leads to great careers in tech. The problem is that it's closed to all but a privileged few. We're blazing an entirely new path, clearing obstacles and inviting trailblazers of all kinds to come with us.

Education should be
a partnership.

Traditionally learning's been a one way street. Schools get paid whether you meet your goals or not; all the risk's on you. We think learning should be a collaboration: a community investing in learners and only making money when they win. That's what drives our program to be 100% focused on giving Learners skills that will lead directly to their success.

Everyone deserves
an opportunity.

Tech's diversity problem isn't getting better. Our mission is to support aspiring developers from all backgrounds gain access to a career in tech.

OUR STORY

It began in 2012 when Shereef Bishay, a programmer and entrepreneur, realized there had to be a better way for his friends to learn to code. At the time they were facing four years in college, hundreds of thousands of dollars in debt and spent little time working on real world projects.

Shereef conceived of a very different experience and invited a few of his friends to join. Called Dev Bootcamp, it was a 10-week immersive program to teach people the skills they need to enter the booming tech sector at a fraction of the cost. It worked, his friends got jobs, and the idea caught on. In fact, within a few years, bootcamps trained tens of thousands of aspiring engineers and gave tech companies a new pipeline for talent.

But Shereef wasn't satisfied. He understood that even though bootcamps are far cheaper than college, they still aren't accessible to most; millions can't afford to take on any debt at all. In fact, millions can't spend time in school not earning an income. Not to mention people of color and women are still greatly under-represented in the tech industry - a problem bootcamps aren't built to solve.

So in 2015, Shereef and some of the best and brightest in the field decided to start something even more radical: a learning community that is both more inclusive and more intensive. Designed for people who have the drive but just need a real opportunity.

Learners Guild pivots away from traditional approaches to education, that aren’t working. We do away with up-front tuition, grades, and busy work that’s disconnected from the real world. In its place, we offer no upfront costs, expert support and guidance, hands-on project-based work, a learning environment that’s safe and collaborative and a commitment to be in your corner all along the way. We’re not just widening the circle of opportunity - we’re widening it and creating an accessible on-ramp to help people like you.
