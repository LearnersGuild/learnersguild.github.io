ABOUT US

We're building this for you.

We believe:

It's time for pioneers.

There's a well-worn path that leads to great careers in tech. The problem is that it's closed to all but a privileged few. We're blazing an entirely new path, clearing obstacles and inviting trailblazers of all kinds come with us.

Education should be
a partnership.

Traditionally learning's been a one way street. Schools get paid whether you meet your goals or not; all the risk's on you. We think learning should be a collaboration: a community investing in learners and only making money when they win. That's what drives our program to be 100% focused on giving Learners skills that will lead directly to their success.

Everyone deserves
an opportunity.

Tech offers high challenge, high reward 21st century careers. But for too many, the on-ramps don't exist. We think commitment, not privilege should determine success. That's why we're building a new on-ram, a supportive and safe environment where everyone can thrive.

Our Story

It began in 2012 when Shereef Bishay, a programmer and entrepreneur, realized there had to be a better way for his friends to learn to code. At the time they were facing four years in college, hundreds of thousands of dollars in debt and spent little time working on real world projects.
