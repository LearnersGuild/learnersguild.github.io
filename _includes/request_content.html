Wait. No Teachers?

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all learning.

If you're looking for a set curriculum like the one you would find at a school or bootcamp, you won't find it at Learners Guild

We believe the best answer to "what should I learn?" is "whatever the most skilled people around you know." So we've created a learning collective--a learners-only apprenticeship program.

You'll be learning skills and receiving guidance from full-stack JavaScript web developers--some of whom are pioneers in the tech world. And within 10 weeks of the program, you'll act as a mentor to a newer cohort of learners, guiding them as they encounter concepts that you've recently mastered.

Learners Guild vs.
College or Bootcamp.

Why should you pick the Learners Guild path to career success over a degree program or an intensive bootcamp?
Here's what sets us apart:

College

  • Traditional CS degree
  • Holistic education, predominantly not skills-based
  • For top-tier high school students
  • 4 years long
  • Schedule depends on class availability
  • $153K
  • Student responsibility (loans) – pays regardless of job outcome
  • Not job-focused

Learners Guild

  • Degree alternative
  • Holistic, skills-based education for software development jobs
  • For anyone
  • 10 months long
  • Weekdays 8:30am - 6:00pm
  • $28K
  • Financed upfront through outside investors – repayment hinges on successful student employment
  • Job-focused

Bootcamp

  • Skills augmentation
  • Quick 'finishing school'
  • Has a degree, looking to brush up
  • 10-12 weeks long
  • Work around the clock
  • $13K
  • Student responsibility (loans) – pays regardless of job outcome
  • Job-focused
The upshot: unlike college or bootcamp students, as a Learners Guild learner
you'll never be in a position where you can't repay a loan.
