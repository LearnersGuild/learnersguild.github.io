Computational thinking is the range of cognitive abilities needed to program a computer. It includes abilities like logical reasoning, algorithm design, pattern recognition, and evaluating a series of sequential instructions.

Why is computational thinking important? Writing programs for computers can be difficult, requiring significant mental effort. Developing good problem-solving strategies and habits of mind will help you move find solutions more quickly and (hopefully) enjoy the challenge of doing so!

The puzzles here are designed to exercise key computational thinking abilities. They don't require any special technical training beyond basic math. If you enjoy this kind of problem solving, you're likely going to enjoy other aspects of programming.

3: Conditionals and logical reasoning

A grocery store uses stickers to categorize and label their goods according to the rules listed here.

Read the rules and then answer the questions.

What would the sticker look like for an imported organic banana that can be purchased with food stamps, but is not on sale?

  1. Green square with #i on the label
  2. Green circle with #i on the label
  3. Blue circle with #L on the label
  4. Green circle with I on the label

What kind of sticker would you use for conventional salmon raised locally that are on sale and available for purchase with food stamps?

Write down your answer so you can review later.

  • Rules for labeling grocery items:
  • For fresh produce (fruits and vegetables), the stickers are circular. For meat, fish, and poultry, the stickers are square. For all other products, the stickers are diamond-shaped.
  • The stickers for organic food are green; for conventional food they are blue.
  • Items that are available for purchase with food stamps have a # printed on their stickers.
  • If the food was produced locally, the sticker will have an l. If it was produced in another state in the US, it will have the letter u. Imported items are demarcated by an i on the sticker.
  • If the item is on sale, all letters on the sticker will be printed in uppercase.
