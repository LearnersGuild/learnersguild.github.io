What to Expect from a Learners Guild Education

Learning for the Real World

The world of a professional software engineer doesn’t involve desks, teachers, books, or tests. So why software development training? At Learners Guild, you’ll build real world software in teams of 3 to 5 learners of various experience levels.

Learning Through Apprenticeship

The best way to learn is side-by-side with professional software developers. Under the guidance of some of the most pioneering senior engineers and practitioners in the tech world, you and your peers will collaborate on creating real-world software and contribute to open source projects. You’ll walk away with a stellar portfolio.

Learning By Mentoring Others

As you advance through the program, you’ll lead groups of newer learners, supporting them as they adopt concepts you’ve recently mastered. You’ll gain leadership skills and put into practice everything you’ve been learning.

Learning Through Belonging

You’ll be part of a diverse group of people with a shared passion for coding. You’ll connect with influential mentors who will challenge you and encourage you. You’ll come away from Learners Guild with all the tools you need for a successful career, including a supportive community of peers.
