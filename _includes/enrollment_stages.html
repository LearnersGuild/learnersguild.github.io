Timeline

1. Submit Application

Request an application for a spot in the July 2016 Oakland cohort and submit your application as soon as possible. Make sure to read through the eligibility requirements.

2. Complete Interview

If you're eligible to join the program, you'll be invited to schedule a brief online interview with someone from our enrollment team.

3. Join An Enrollment Game

Select applicants will be invited to join a full-day Enrollment Game, where they'll meet and collaborate with other applicants and experience a taste of life at Learners Guild.

4. Cohort Selection

After reviewing applications and feedback from the enrollment games, we'll notify all applicants of their acceptance status. If offered a spot in the Guild, you'll be notified at least one month before the Oakland cohort start date (July 11).
