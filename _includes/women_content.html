Wait. No Teachers?

We don't believe in one-size-fits-all learning.

If you're looking for a set curriculum like the one you would find at a school or bootcamp, you won't find it at Learners Guild

We believe the best answer to "what should I learn?" is "whatever the most skilled people around you know." So we've created a learning collective--a learners-only apprenticeship program.

You'll be learning skills and receiving guidance from full-stack JavaScript web developers--some of whom are pioneers in the tech world. And within 10 weeks of the program, you'll act as a mentor to a newer cohort of learners, guiding them as they encounter concepts that you've recently mastered.

Have a Life Outside of Learning

We meet every weekday from 8:30am - 6:00pm.

You shouldn’t have to sacrifice all of your free time or your family’s well-being while getting an immersive education. At Learners Guild, you’ll have time to spend with your family and friends so you can return the next day refreshed and ready to learn.

Nothing To Lose. Everything to Gain.

Getting an education should never be a risk.

You shouldn't have to go into tens of thousands of dollars of debt before you even earn a penny in the career you're training for.

And you shouldn't have to worry about how you're going to pay the bills or support your family while you study to earn a better livelihood.

So here is our guarantee: we'll cover your program fees and $1500 per month of your living expenses for the duration of the program. Only when you've obtained a full-time position in a development career will we ask you to pay us back.

No risks, no worry, no debt. Period.
